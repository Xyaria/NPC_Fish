# Mod introduction event

Trigger condition: Lvl 2 fishing
Location: Farm
Actors: Farmer, Willy
Script: 
	Farmer leaves farmhouse
	Willy stand in front of the door
	Willy: ($2) Ahoy lad/lass/kid...
	Farmer emote [?]
	<Q: He looks concerned.
		1- Are you ok?
		2- What's wrong?
	>
	Pause
	Willy turns down
	Willy emote [drop]
	Willy: ($0) I'll ask you to trust me on this one.
	Willy: ($2) There is a... weird fish, that looks like me, over by the beach.
	Willy: ($2) It had a beret and a fishing rod!
	Pause
	Farmer emote [!]
	Farmer animation [laugh]
	Willy turns right
	Willy emote [grumble]
	Willy: ($2) That's not a joke! And yes, I was as sober as one can be!
	Pause
	Willy turns up
	Willy: ($0) Listen lad/lass/kid. That fish genuinely scares me.
	Willy: ($0) I came to ask you to catch it for me.
	Willy: ($1) You've become quite the good angler now!
	Willy: ($2) Anyway. If you do catch it... Honestly I don't care, I don't want to see it.
	Farmer emote [...]
	Fade
	<He looked really distraught.>
	<A weird fish that looked like Willy? You're really curious to see it, if it exists!>

# Mod Introduction Quest

Title: A weird fish?
Reward: 1000g
Content: 
	Willy told you about a strange fish "that looked like him", which he saw at the beach by his house.
	You should try to catch it - even just to see if it does exist.

# Mod Introduction Letter

Trigger condition: Completed "A weird fish?" quest
Content:
	Greetings <farmer>.

	I concur Willy told you about the "weird fish" he spotted? He told everyone actually, even told us you caught it. Honestly, congrats.

	He had setup a book in his house so we can all report sighting of this fish, but it ended up being filled with sightings of a lot of *different* weird fish.
	Don't bother going however: it mysteriously disappeared the other day. I think I heard Penny said she spotted one of the pages torn from it and floating in the wind... You might find them all around the valley.

	If you want to go and try to catch them all, well... We'll all be quite happy. These strange fish are scaring everyone!

	- Lewis
